Alice is a young mathematician who loves collecting numbers. One day, she lined up $n$ of her favourite integers in a row, forming an array $a_1, a_2, \ldots, a_n$. She also chose a special number called the \textit{target}.

Alice wonders whether two of her numbers, at different positions, secretly add up to the target. She asks you: can you find two indices $i$ and $j$ ($i \neq j$) such that $a_i + a_j = \text{target}$?
