To make a query, print a single integer $m$ ($1 \leq m \leq n$) and flush output.
The judge will respond with one of:

\begin{itemize}
    \item \t{less} --- the hidden value $x < m$,
    \item \t{greater} --- the hidden value $x > m$,
    \item \t{equal} --- you found $x$; the interaction ends.
\end{itemize}

You may ask at most $30$ queries in total.
If you exceed $30$ queries or print an invalid value, the judge will respond \t{wrong} and the verdict is Wrong Answer.
